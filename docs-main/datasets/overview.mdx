## Dataset Selection Framework

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define Application Requirements">
    **Medical Education Applications:** General Medical Instruction, Medical Knowledge Cards, Licensing Examination datasets
    
    **Clinical Consultation Systems:** Clinical Consultation Dataset, Healthcare Professional Consultation collections
    
    **Clinical Decision Support:** Clinical Decision Intelligence, Medical Ontology & Relations datasets
    
    **Pharmaceutical Applications:** Pharmaceutical Knowledge Dataset with specialized drug information
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Assess Technical Requirements">
    **Compact Implementations:** Medical Knowledge Cards, Pharmaceutical Knowledge datasets
    
    **Comprehensive Applications:** General Medical, Clinical Consultation, Healthcare Professional datasets
    
    **Enterprise Solutions:** Combined dataset collections with full spectrum coverage
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Evaluate Integration Complexity">
    **Structured Responses:** Medical Assessment Dataset, Medical Knowledge Cards
    
    **Detailed Clinical Content:** General Medical Instruction, Healthcare Professional Consultation
    
    **Conversational Interfaces:** Clinical Consultation Dataset
    
    **Semantic Applications:** Medical Ontology & Relations, Medical Research & Evidence
  </Step>
</Steps>
